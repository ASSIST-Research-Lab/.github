# ASSIST Research Lab @ Florida Tech
### **Assured Safety, Security and Intent with Systematic Tactics**

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of the **ASSIST Research Lab** at the Florida Institute of Technology. Directed by **Dr. Siddhartha Bhattacharyya**, our lab is dedicated to advancing the frontier of assurance frameworks for mission-critical, safety-critical, and security-critical systems.

[**Explore Our Research**](https://research.fit.edu/assist-lab/) • [**Our Publications**](https://research.fit.edu/assist-lab/publications/) • [**Meet the Team**](https://research.fit.edu/assist-lab/people/)

## Our Mission
The ASSIST Lab focuses on the design, development, and deployment of robust assurance frameworks. We integrate theories of computation, formal methods, model-based engineering, architecture analysis, and software/systems engineering principles to systematically verify and assure the correctness of complex systems.

Our research spans critical domains where failure is not an option, bridging the gap between rigorous academic theory and real-world industrial application.

---

## Core Research Pillars

| Focus Area | Key Technologies & Concepts | Applications |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Formal Verification** | Formal Methods, Automata Theory, Proof Assistants, Temporal Logic | Mission-critical avionics, behavior assurance |
| **Model-Based Systems Engineering** | SysML, AADL, Architecture Analysis, Physics-Based Reasoning | Correct-by-construction systems design |
| **Autonomous & Cyber-Physical Systems** | Safe AI/ML Integration, ROS, Control Loop Verification | UAVs, robotics, self-driving architectures |
| **Cybersecurity & IoT Privacy** | Network Security, Formal Protocol Analysis, Threat Modeling | Secure communication, edge IoT safety |
